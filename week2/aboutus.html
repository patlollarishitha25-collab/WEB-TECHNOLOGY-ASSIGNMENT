ABOUT US

Vardhaman College of Engineering is a premier institute of higher learning in the field of engineering and technology. Established in 1999, the college has been providing quality education and fostering innovation and research among its students.


The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines of engineering, including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering. With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life, Vardhaman College of Engineering aims to nurture the next generation of engineers who can contribute to society and industry.
